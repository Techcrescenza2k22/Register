About the department


The Department of Information Technology (IT) was established in the year 2001 with the aim of providing inclusive technology education to students, and thereby creating responsible citizens who would be a globally competent individuals contributing to the betterment of their families, society and nation. IT department started with UG Program with a sanctioned intake of 60 students. The Department has the state-of-the-art facilities for various laboratories, a well-equipped seminar hall, Wi-Fi enabled campus to support e-Learning, and a department library.
