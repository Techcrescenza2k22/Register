Memory League

Rounds ✅

Round 1 - Visual Hunt

  1. Participants will be shown with collage of images to capture it for few seconds.👀💭
  2. They will be questioned based on their observation.⁉️
    Eg: count of particular item,duplicate item...

Round 2 - Memory train

  1. Chain of 6️⃣to 8️⃣ images will be displayed to the participants.
  2. After the close watch of the images,the participants should write down the order of pictures which was shown.📝💯

Round 3 - bric-a-brac

  1. Participants will be given a tongue twister to be remembered. 💬
  2. Nextly,they will have to play a small game (battle with Balloon) 🎈💫
  3. The team which finished the game first and tells the exact tongue twister will be the winner🎖🎉✨️

Rewards 🎉

  1. Top 3 teams will be awarded CASH PRIZE and all participants will awarded with participation certificate and small token of love.🎁💓

Rules 📜

  1. Maximum 2 members in a team
  2. Team which qualifies all 3 round will be the ultimate winners.
  3. Each round will have elimination.
